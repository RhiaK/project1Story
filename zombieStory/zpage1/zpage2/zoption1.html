Page Two

It is best if I explore the house before making myself comfortable, maybe it still has running water. I am new to this street, maybe it wasn't shut off like that place I hid in last night.

I'm optomistic as I enter the kitchen. I think it takes a certain level of optimism to survive this new world. I walk very quietly, there is no guarantee that this place isn't infested with zombies. The kitchen is clear, I try the faucet on the sink. The water turns on! it even runs clear!

I am so excited right now, I can barely contain myself. I retreive my water bottle form my pack, fill it and return it to my backpack to make sure I don't forget it.

Scream in delight, I have wanted running water for so long!
Rush upstairs for a shower as fast as I can
Clear the rest of the house first, then take a shower
