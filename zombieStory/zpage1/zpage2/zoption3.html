Page Two

The zombie is passed the house and I have no idea what could be in this place. I am getting out of here right now!

I silently open the door and slip out. I walk quietly and slowly, crouching behind any object I can find as I move back down the street, heading north. It is slow going, but better safe than sorry when you know there is a zombie around. As I duck behind a car, my foot slips on a greasy wrapper on the ground.

"oooomph!" The sound comes out involuntarily. Oh no, could a zombie be near enough to have heard me?

Get up and run as fast as I can
Assume no one heard me and keep going as planned
