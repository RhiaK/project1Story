I reach under the bush, slowly reaching towards the puppy so I don't startle it. The last thing I need is for it to bite me out of fear. The puppy lets me pick it up, it must have belonged to a family, it doesn't seem afraid of me at all. I look at its little paw and see that it seems swollen, I'm not sure he can walk. He's so small though, he could easily fit in my backpack.

I have no idea what kind of puppy he is but it would sure be nice to not be alone all the time and he hasn't barked this whole time. What should I do?
