The End of All Things

Page Four

I am so ready for bed. I head to the bedroom that seemed the most comfy and enter it. I push the dresser up against the back of the door, just in case.

I curl up in bed, ready for a good night's sleep. This is a luxury I haven't had in what feels like forever. I think of my family that I was seperated from a week ago and hope they are safe as I drift off to sleep.

In the morning, I wake feeling rested, happy from an uninterrupted night's sleep.

Go scout the kitchen for some food
Clear the rest of the house, just in case
Leave the house and be on my way, best be getting on
