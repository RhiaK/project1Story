The End of All Things

Page Four

There is a row of townhomes off to the left. A few of them have broken windows and don't look to be in the best condition, but the one on the end looks pretty good. I go and try opening the door, it's unlocked! I head in.

I look around and it seems safe. I am just so tired, I sit on the couch for a moment before clearing the house, to make sure I am alert and ready.

I swear I only close my eyes for a second, but the next thing I know, there is a zombie on top of me making weird noises right before it bites down. I try to fight it off, but there is another right behind it. I close my eyes so I don't have to watch as I succumb to death.
