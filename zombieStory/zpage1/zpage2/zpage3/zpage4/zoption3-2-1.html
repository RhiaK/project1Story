Page Four

I start checking car doors to find one that is unlocked. I luck out on the third car I try. It's even an SUV with tinted windows. I couldn't have found a better car to spend the night in!

I lay out the clothes in my backpack to use as a pillow, making my nest in the back of the SUV. I make sure all the doors are locked and drift off to sleep. I sleep pretty solidly through the night and only wake when the sunshine starts streaming through the windows.
