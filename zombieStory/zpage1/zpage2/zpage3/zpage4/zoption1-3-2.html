I am so ready for bed. I head ot the bedroom that seemed the most comfy and push the dresser up against the back of the door, just in case.

I curl up in bed, ready for a good night's sleep. This is a luxury I haven't had in what feels like forever. I think of my family that I was seperated from a week ago and hope they are safe as I drift off to sleep.

In the morning, I wake feeling rested, happy for an uninterrupted night's sleep.
