I head straight to the kitchen, maybe there is some food left that hasn't gone bad yet. I check the pantry first and find a couple cans of beans and a can of peaches, all of them appear to be a bit dented, might be why they were left behind.

It's probably best if I don't eat anything out of these cans, I really cannot afford to get sick right now and I know dented cans can be contaminated with bacteria. I guess I'll just head to bed, afterall...
