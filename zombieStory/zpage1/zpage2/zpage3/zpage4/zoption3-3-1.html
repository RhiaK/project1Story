The End of All Things

Page Four

I go to the nearest house, it doesn't look too safe, but I don't have time to be picky right now. I need to get this bite cleaned. Maybe if I can disinfect it quickly enough, it will stop the infection.

Under the sink in the kitchen I find a first aid kit with hydrogen peroxide inside. I pour all of it over the wound, no need to be stingy now. I smear some neosporin on it and bandage it up. I guess it's the best I can do, I just hope it's enough.

I lie down for some rest, I am just so tired.....I don't feel so good.......
