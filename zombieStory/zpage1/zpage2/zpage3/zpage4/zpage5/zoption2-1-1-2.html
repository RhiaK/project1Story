The End of All Things

Page Five

I run into the nearest house, thankfully the door is unlocked. I rush straight to the kitchen, looking for a first aid kit. I find one under the sink and pour the disinfectant on my scratch. I bandage it up, hoping that it will keep the infection at bay. It's the best I can do for now....

I head upstairs to find a bedroom for the night. I push the dresser up against the door and make myself comfortable on the giant bed. I am so ready for a decent night's sleep. I awake in the morning after sleeping better than I have in awhile, but I still feel a bit tired, I hope its not from the scratch....
