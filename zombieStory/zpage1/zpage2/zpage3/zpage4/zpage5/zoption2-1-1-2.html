Page Five

I run into the nearest house on the right, thankfully the door is unlocked. I rush straight to the kitchen, looking for a first aid kit. I find it under the sink and pour disinfectant on my scratch. I bandage it up, hoping that it will keep the infection at bay.

I head upstairs to find a bedroom for the night. I push the dresser up against the door and make myself comfortable. I am so ready for a decent night's sleep. I awake in the morning after sleeping better than I have in awhile.
