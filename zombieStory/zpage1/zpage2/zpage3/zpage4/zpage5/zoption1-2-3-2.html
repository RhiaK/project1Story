The End of All Things

Page Five

I know I shouldn't stay in one place for too long but it feels like forever since I got to just relax for a while. I decide to hang out in the house for the day, maybe I'll get back on the road tomorrow.

I find a book and get lost in the story when I hear glass breaking in the front room. I jump up, grab my hammer and carefully enter the front room. A zombie is climbing in through the broken window.

I rush him with my hammer at the ready and right as I'm about to bring it down, he moves low and bites me in the stomach. It surprises me to much that I fall while the zombie falls on top of me and keeps biting. I close my eyes as evertyhing gets colder and colder, wishing this wasn't the way I would go out...
