The End of All Things

Page Five

I find a can opener in the drawer and open the cans, the beans smell a bit funny but I eat them anyway. You never know when you will find more food, I can't afford to be picky. The peaches are the best thing I've eaten in forever

Before too long, my stomach starts feeling uneasy, maybe I ate too fast. I rest for a bit on the couch. I am feeling worse and worse. I think I'm going to have to stay put for at least another night. Wait till I get better.

The next morning I am still curled up on the couch in pain. I feel too weak to move or do anything else.

I hear glass shatter from the window in the living room. I am too weak to get up as the zombie that just broke in gets nearer and nearer. I lay on the couch weakly as the zombie begins to devour me.
