Page Five

I slow down, but keep moving. I begin my stealth routine, ducking behind cars and trying to blend into the shadows as I move slowly down the street.

I keep going like this for about another hour, but I am just getting too tired to keep moving. I should look for somewhere to rest for the night, there is only so far one person can travel on foot.

I round the next corner, doing my best to keep moving north. I am just so tired, but right as I finish a yawn, I see a smallish zombie running right towards me. I grab my hammer out of my belt loop right as it scratches me. I manage to take it down, but will I be infected now?
