The End of All Things

Page Five

I leave the house to continue my trek north, towards my family's cabin. I move quickly, but carefully up the street. I make good time, probably because I am so well rested.

As I enter the forest I can barely contain my excitement. I move closer to the cabin and worry that I won't find my family there. All the fears I have chosen not to think about, come to the surface. What if they went somewhere else? What if they didn't make it out? What if they got turned into zombies?

I see the cabin through the trees right up ahead.
