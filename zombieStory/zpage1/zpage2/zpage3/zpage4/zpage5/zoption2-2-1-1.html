The End of All Things

Page Five

I have been so lonely, I know it might be risky, but this puppy is just too cute. I decide I am going to try to keep him. I load him up into my backpack, leaving the top open so he can peek out and get enough air.

I continue on my trek north to the family cabin. I keep it stealthy, sneaking behind the cars on the side of the road, trying to slink through the shadows. Right as I step out from onw shadow to make my way to the next, the puppy yips, loudly.

I turn to look behind me and see a fast moving zombie right about to crash into us. I move out of the way as I grab my hammer right as the puppy tries to jump out of the backpack on my back, he throws my balance off and I fall. The zombie lunges for me and begins tearing into me. At least I won't die alone I think as I watch the puppy run under the nearest car. The last thing I see are his sad puppy eyes.
