The End of All Things

Page Five

I go through the house, room by room, as quietly as I can, hammer in hand, and don't find any other, living (or non-living), creature in the house. I'm safe for now.

I check the bedrooms for any clean clothes that might be useful, or any other supplies and make a pile in the living room. I take my time repacking my stuff and relax for a little bit.

My family always warned me about staying in any one place for too long, so I head out around lunch time to continue my trek north towards the family cabin.
