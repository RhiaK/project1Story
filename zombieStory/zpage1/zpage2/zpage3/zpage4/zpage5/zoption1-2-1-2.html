The End of All Things

Part One: Page Five

I go through the house, room to room, as quietly as I can, hammer in hand, and don't find any other, living (or non-living), person in the house. Safe for now.

I check the bedrooms for any clean clothes that might be useful, or any other supplies and make a pile in the living room. I take my time repacking my stuff and relax for a little bit.

My family always warned about staying any one place for too long, so I head out around lunch time to continue my trek north.

Take as direct a route as possible, moving as quickly as I can
Be as stealthy as possible, slow and steady wins the race
