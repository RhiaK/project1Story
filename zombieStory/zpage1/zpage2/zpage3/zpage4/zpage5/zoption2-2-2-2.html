The End of All Things

Page Five

I duck behind the nearest car, hoping the zombies don't notice me as they shamble down the road, hoarde style. I do my best to make zero noise, but I still smell pretty bad. I hope they aren't able to smell me from where they are.

I notice one zombie slowly pulling away from the group, it is coming nearer to me and sniffing the air. I really should have found a way to take a shower earlier. I keep still and hope the zombie will lose interest.

The zombie seems to get excited and it starts to run towards me, making grunting noises that alert the other zombies to my presence. I try to get up and run away, but they are quick and overtake me. I scream as I fall, surrounded by dozens of hungry zombies.
