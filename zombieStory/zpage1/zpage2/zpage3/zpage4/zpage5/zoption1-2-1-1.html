The End of All Things

Page Five

I head down to the kitchen to see if I can find anything to eat. I think my last meal was lunch yesterday, if you could call a handful of crakers and some candy, lunch.

I find a couple cans of beans and a can of peaches in the pantry, but they all look pretty dented, that might be why they were left behind. After a thorough search of the rest of the kitchen, it appears the place has been picked clean. I have a little food left in my backpack, but I have no idea where I might find more.
