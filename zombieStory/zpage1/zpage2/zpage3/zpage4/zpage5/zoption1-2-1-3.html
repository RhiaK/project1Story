The End of All Things

Page Five

Feeling very refreshed, it's time to get going. The sooner I get back on the road the sooner I can get to the cabin and maybe my family. I move quickly north and manage to make really good time. I don't run into any zombies on the way and before I know it, I am in the same forrest as the family cabin.

I am so excited and so tired of being all alone. I know it's only been a week but without an other person but those trying to eat you, it gets really lonely.

I start to get a little nervous as I get nearer and nearer to the cabin. I can see it peeking through the trees up ahead. I see smoke coming from the chimny.
