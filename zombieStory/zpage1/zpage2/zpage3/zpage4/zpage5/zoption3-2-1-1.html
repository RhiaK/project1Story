Page Five

I feel so much better after getting a decent night's sleep, even if it was cramped up in the back of an SUV. I pack up my stuff, make sure my hammer is in my belt loop, and head out.

I keep moving north, hoping to make it to the family cabin. I am feeling pretty good so I make decent time and manage to not run into any zombies. The hoardes don't seem to be happening as often as they have the past month.

I start getting really excited as I enter the forest, just a few more miles and I will be at the cabin. I see a zombie just a bit ahead and manage to sneak up on it and take it out with my trusty hammer.

As I continue moving northward, I start seeing the cabin peak through the trees. What should I do?
