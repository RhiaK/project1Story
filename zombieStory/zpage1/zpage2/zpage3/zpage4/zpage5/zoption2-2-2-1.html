Page Five

I sneak past them by sticking to the shadows and moving as quietly as I can. Once I am a few blocks away I start to feel a lot better, I guess they didn't notice me afterall.

I am pretty tired but seeing all those zombies woke me up a bit, I guess adrenaline will do that to you. I keep heading north, making good time as I go. Before I know it, I can see the forest up ahead that houses the family cabin.

I keep going and soon, come upon the cabin itself. I can't believe I made such good time, to finally be here after a week all alone. I just hope my family has also made it to the cabin.
