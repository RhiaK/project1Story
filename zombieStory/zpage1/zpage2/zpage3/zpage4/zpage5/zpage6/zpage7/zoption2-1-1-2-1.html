The End of All Things

Page Six

I head out, ready to get back on the road. My leg feels like its starting to burn. I stop for a moment, peel back the bandage and take a look at the scratch from the night before.

It is swollen and red. I am not sure what else to do but get to my family cabin as quickly as possible and hope that someone there can help me.

I see the cabin ahead in the clearing and run up and open the door, I am so done with being alone. When I open the door I see a group of zombies come racing at me. As they bite me and I fade out, I realize this is my family. I was too late I guess my scratch doesn't matter too much afterall...bbbBBBBRRRRAAAIIIINNSSSSS!!!!
