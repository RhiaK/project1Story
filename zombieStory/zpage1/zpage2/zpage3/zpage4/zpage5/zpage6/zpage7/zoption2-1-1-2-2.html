The End of All Things

Page Six

I hang out in the house for awhile, its been forever since I could just relax. I read for a couple hours, then head out, ready to get back on the road despite the scratch on my leg burning. Maybe someone at the cabin can help...

I travel fairly slowly due to the ache in my leg, but see the forest up ahead before I know it. As I approach the cabin in the woods, I nearly squeal with delight, but manage to hold back the urge.

I see the cabin ahead in the clearing and run up and open the door, I am so done with being alone. When I open the door I see a group of zombies come racing at me. As they bite me and I fade out, I realize this is my family. I was too late, I guess the scratch doesn't matter afterall...bbbBBBBRRRRAAAIIIINNSSSSS!!!!
