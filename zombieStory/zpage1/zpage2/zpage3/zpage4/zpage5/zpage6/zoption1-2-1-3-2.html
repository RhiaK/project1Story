The End of All Things

Page Six

I see the cabin through the trees right up ahead, I settle in to wait for an hour or so. I want to make sure it is safe, not just rush in without a thought.

After I feel it's been long enough I get up, excited to finally see my family again, I haven't seen anything out of the ordinary during my wait. As I near the cabin, I am attacked by a zomie, right there in the woods only steps form my goal. It takes me by surprise because I was too busy looking at the cabin and ends me very quickly with a well-placed bite to the throat.
