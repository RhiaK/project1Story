The End of All Things

Page six

I slowly approach the girl, she seems to be mostly still so I don't think it's a huge risk. I walk as quietly as I can, trying not to alert her to my presence. As I get nearer, I notice a smell coming from her. I wanted so badly to not be alone anymore, but I guess it is too late for her.

I am just about to begin my retreat when she reaches out and grabs my arm, pulling me in for a big bite. I try to yank my arm away, but she's so much stronger than the zombies I am used to. It must be because she is so new, I think, as she tears the life out of me.
