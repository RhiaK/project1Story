The End of All Things

Page Six

I can't risk eating the food, not when the cans look that bad. I can't afford to get sick, not now. So I pack up my stuff and head out. I continue travelling north and make good time. I don't run into anyone at all on my way. The forest lies ahead.

As I enter the forest I can barely contian my excitement. I move closer to the cabin and worry that I won't find my family there. All the fears I have chosen not to think about, come to the surface. What if they went somewhere else? What if they didn't make it out? What if they got turned into zombies?

I see the cabin through the trees right up ahead, but as I near it, I am attacked by a zomie, right there in the woods only steps from my goal. It takes me by surprise, I was too busy looking at the cabin and the zombie ends me quickly with a well-placed bite to the throat.
