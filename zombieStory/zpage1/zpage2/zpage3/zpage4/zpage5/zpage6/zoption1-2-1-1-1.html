The End of All Things

Page Six

I am so hungry, the food is probaly okay. I find a can opener in one of the drawers and pop open all the cans. The beans taste a little weird, but the peaches are the best thing I've eaten in some time.

I go sit on the couch after eating, I'm not feeling too good. As time passes I feel worse and worse. I end up stranded on the couch, too weak to move. I left my water bottle in the kitchen and can't bring myself to go get it. I sleep for awhile, off and on.

When I wake for the last time, I have no idea what day it is and find that I have soiled myself, repetedly by the looks and smell of it. I feel weaker than I ever have before. I close my eyes and go to sleep for the last time.
