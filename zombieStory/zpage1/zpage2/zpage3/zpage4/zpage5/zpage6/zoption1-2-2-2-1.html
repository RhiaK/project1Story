The End of All Things

Page Six

I travel fairly slowly but see the forest up ahead before I know it. As I approach the cabin in the woods, I nearly squeal with delight, but manage to hold back the urge.

I see the cabin ahead in the clearing and run up and open the door, I am so done with being alone. When I open the door I see a group of zombies come racing at me. As they bite me and I fade out, I realize this is my family. I was too late...
