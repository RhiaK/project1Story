Page Five

I try my best to clear my head of anything zombie related and just enjoy the peace and quiet. I find myself drifting back to sleep. I'm not terribly tired, but just so comfortable and warm.

I wake in the early afternoon, pack up and start back on my trek northward. I am making pretty good time and soon see the forest up ahead. Only a few miles in lies the family cabin. My excitement at potentially seeing my family again is building as I near the cabin. Then I see it ahead through the trees.
