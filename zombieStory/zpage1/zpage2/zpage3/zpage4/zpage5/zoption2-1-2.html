The End of All Things

Page Five

As I slow down and start to relax, I notice a building on the left side of the road. It is a large brick building, maybe a warehouse or something. It could be a very safe place to lay low for a bit. I have had about enough of dealing with my own grossness and might be able to find a shower in here.

I open the door to a large dark room, I can barely see, it is so dark inside. There is a moaning noise coming from a little to the right, behind the door. I try to get a better look. Nothing has jumped out, so it should be safe.

I reach in my backpack and get out a flashlight that I try not to use, to save the battery. I shine it on the noise and see a girl with bites all over her. She appears to be in pain and is probably not far from turning into a zombie herself.

Get out of here as quickly as possible, I'll find somewhere else to sleep
Try to help the girl, see if she still has enough humanity in her to talk to me
