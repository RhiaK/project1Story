The End of All Things

Page Three

My heart is racing, this is exactly the sort of mistake that could get me killed. I jump up and just start running north, the direction I was travelling to begin with, towards my family cabin. I just run as fast as I can, hoping to outpace anything that could have heard me.

As I round a corner, I smack face-first into something solid. I look up and am met with the partially-decomposed face of a zombie.

I fall back in fright as the zombie falls right on top of me and starts tearing chunks of my flesh off. I feel nothing but pain till the cold starts to seep through. I close my eyes, never to open them again...
