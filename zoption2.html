In this new world, it always pays to play it safe. I will stay here, right by the door and wait to make sure the zombie doesn't return and the coast remains clear, then head on my way.

I peek through the window in the door, nothing outside moves, I don't hear any noises coming fro the house. Now is as good a time as any, i guess. I silently open the door and slip out with none the wiser.

I run down the street as quietly as I can, hoping to get out of the area as quickly as possible. I hate sticking around an area after I see a zombie roaming. Just as I get to the nearest intersection, I hear something rustling in the bushes off to the right.
