The End of All Things

Part One

Running again, it feels like I am always running these days. I run a grubby hand through my hair, tucking it behind my ear. It is so greasy.

"ewwww" I think as I remember that it's been about a week since the last shower I had. I hope I can find a safe place with running water again soon. As I round a corner, I duck into the nearest house, hoping that my luck is still with me. I see the zombie that was chasing me keep going down the street. I am safe for now.

I feel a surge of relief as I turn around to check out the house I now find myself in.
