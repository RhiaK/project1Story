Out in Space

Page Two

I could totally check out Jupiter's moon, but so can anyone else. I think I am going to go for the further request. It sounds a lot more exotic and a better use of my time. I set the coordinates in my system and get ready for a long sleep.

When I awake, I find that an alarm is sounding. I am not sure how long it has been going off, or if I was even supposed to wake just yet. I turn my seat so that I am facing my instrument panel and check the message from the alarm system.

It seems that I was hit by a meteoroid on my way to my destination. There is some slight external damage to my ship, but it might hold, or I could head out and try to fix it. I was trained for this afterall....
