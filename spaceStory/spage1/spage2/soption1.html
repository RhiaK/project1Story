Page Two

I should check out Jupiter's moon first, maybe I can even do it on the way to the other request. Kill two birds with one stone. I set the coordinates in my instrument panel like I have done hundreds of times in training, but this time I am really going somewhere, I am so excited!

Off to Europa it is! Humans have still been unable to determine whether the giant ocean below Europa's icy crust sustains any life-forms, and if it does, I want to know what kind of life thrives there. It could be a very interesting discovery.

I land my craft on Jupiter's second moon, Europa. We are sitting on the ice and must drill through it to reach the vast oceans beneath. I bring up my maps of Europa and try to find the best point for drilling. There are two that seem to be good possiblities. One in closer, but thicker, the other farther, but thinner ice. Which should I drill?
