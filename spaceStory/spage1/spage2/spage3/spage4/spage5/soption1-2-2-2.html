I don't think it'll be the best idea to eat the food, it could make me sick and I can't afford to get sick right now. I head upstairs and go to bed. It's been too long since I slept in a comfy bed. I make sure to put the dresser in front of the door to barricade myself in before I fall asleep.

I wake in the morning to the sun shining in through the windows. I feel refreshed and ready for a new day. I climb out the bed and begin getting ready for the day. I move the dresser out of the way and head downstairs.
