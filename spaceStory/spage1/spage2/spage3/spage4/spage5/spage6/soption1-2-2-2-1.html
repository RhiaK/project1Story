Out in Space

Page Six

There is not a whole lot more I can do in this situation without Bo. I decide to wait a bit longer. I start out sitting, but get restless and start pacing. I still have a good deal of oxygen left, so I'm not too worried. As I turn back around to being my slow pace back to the drill site I see something peeking in the hole I drilled. It jumps back and looks at me, surprise all over its face. Or at least I think it's surprise, I have never seen a face like this before. Bo is standing right beside it. I am so happy to see him unhurt. I guess he was off with the aliens.

The alien comes closer to me and holds its hand out, I put my hand out in the same way and we touch. I am so happy that I always leave my camera on, just in case. The people back home are going to love this video.
