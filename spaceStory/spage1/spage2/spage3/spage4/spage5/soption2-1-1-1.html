Out in Space

Page Five

I decide it is better to wait for the samples from the other planets to see which might be the best planet to explore first. The samples come back slowly over the next few hours, but none show any hope of life other than the 2nd planet.

I go visit the second planet, hopeful that it will be flourishing with life. When I land I see nothing but a rocky desert. That doesn't have to mean anything though, plenty of life is microscopic or subterranean. I start doing some more testing.

I am so dissapointed, my tests return nothing interesting. It seems that there is no life on this planet, or in this solar system at all. I go ahead and return to my ship.

Well I could choose to be upset, or I could see this as marking one solar system off my list and get ready to head to the next.
