Out in Space

Page Five

I ask Bo to retrieve another sample. I analyze this one too and come back with the same results. It is still inconclusive. I guess I will have to get lowered down afterall. I get my suit strapped up to Bo so that he can lower me down through the hole. It's a little scary, but kind of exciting too!

My first view of the ocean beneath the ice is magnificent. The waters appear clear and go on forever in each direction. I can't see any life forms but there might be something below the water. My suit can be completely submerged and still keep me safe, comforatble, and best yet, oxygenated.

I keep going down, down, down.... I make sure all my lights are fully functional and I am recording so I can see what's going on in the water around me. I see movement off to the left. Over there!!! It's something swimming, unlike anything I have ever seen before! I did it! I discovered alien life!!!!!
