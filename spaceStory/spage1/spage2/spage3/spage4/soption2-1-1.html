Out in Space

Page Four

I decide it will be a better idea to monitor the solar system for awhile before deciding which planet might house life. I send out a bunch of sensors to gather samples that I can process once they get back to the ship.

I kill some time catching up on my reading, getting a bite to eat, and watching one of my favorite movies. When the first of the samples gets back, I'm just itching for something to do.

I start processing it and find that the planet nearest me contains no life what-so-ever, no water, frozen or otherwise, and no oxygen. I decide it is best that I chose to monitor this solar system before making a rash decision.

The next sample that comes back shows promise. This planet has water but no trace of life in the samples brought back by the sensors. It's inconclusive. Should I wait and keep testing, or go check out this planet?
