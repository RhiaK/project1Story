I get back to the ship in good time, replenish my air supply, always better safe than sorry, grab a bit to eat and head right back over to the drill site.

I test the sample for general composition, but also for any signs of life. None in the sample, hmmmm.... should I get another sample, or have Bo lower me through the hole to get a look around?
