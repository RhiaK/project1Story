I get back to the ship in good time and replenish my air supply, always better safe than sorry. I grab a bite to eat and head right back over to the drill site.

By the time I get there, it looks like the drilling has completed, I lead a water-collection tube through the hole to bring up a sample to test. Good thing I brought my kit.

I test the sample for general composition, but also for any signs of life. No signs of life in this sample, hmmmm.... should I get another sample, or have Bo lower me through the hole to get a look around?
