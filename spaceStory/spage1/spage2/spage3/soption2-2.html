Out in Space

Page Three

I better fix it, it's a little scary just being out here in space, but in space with a broken ship? No thank you!

I get my suit on and head out the air lock. I always feel a little uncomfortable being so explosed to the vacuum of space like this, but I was trained for this very problem. I grab my toolbelt on my way out and make sure I am securely anchored to the ship.

I really hate this, this is the one part of being a space explorer that I absolutely hate. I do my very best to grin and bear it, but right as I am reaching for a tool on my tool belt, I lose my grip on the side of my ship. I freak out for a min, then realize I just need to pull myself back by the cord anchoring me to the ship.

I start pulling, but right then a very small meteroite comes crashing right into my cord. My anchor cord is cut in half and I am spinning aimlessly in the vacuum of space.
