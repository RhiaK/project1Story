Out in Space

Page Three

I go with the spot closer to the ship. It might take a little longer to drill, but what time I lose drilling, will be made up for by not having to travel so far. I'm sure the drill can take it.

I suit up and head out, Bo is able to transport the drill for me, he follows along and we begin drilling not very far from the ship, nice and close to home. After a few hours in, we still have a ways to go.

The ice is harder than I expected and the drill bits have been having to be replaced far too often. At this rate I may be out of bits before I can finish this mission.

CCCRRRRAAAACKKK!!!! I hear as the ice starts to crack, oh no! my ship is too close!. It begins to fall in the crack and I watch as my home, my life-boat in this barren land, is lost forever.....
