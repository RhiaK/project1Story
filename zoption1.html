The End of All Things

Part One: Page Two

It is best if I explore the house, maybe it still has running water. I am new to this street, maybe it wasn't shut off like that last place I was hiding out.

I am optomistic as I enter the kitchen. I walk very quietly, there is no guarantee that this place isn't infested with zombies. The kitchen is clear, I try to faucet at the sink. The water turns on! it even runs clear!

I am so excited right now, I can barely contain myself. I retreive my water bottle form my pack, fill it and return it.

Scream in delight, I have wanted running water for so long!
Rush upstairs for a shower as fast as I can
Clear the rest of the house first, then take a shower
